Manager asks the employee for overtime work. The employee accepts or rejects this proposal. After that the manager receives a notification of acceptance or rejection.

How to play the business process:
  1. Login as member of manager group (e.g. julius)
  2. Click menu item “Process Definitions”
  3. Click “Start” for “Overtime work demo” button
  4. Start form will appear
  • Choose an employee, which will receive proposal for overtime work (e.g. gaiua)
  • Enter the time interval for the overtime work (e.g. 14.03.2006 22:30 - 20.12.2005 23:30)
  • Fill in the fields “reason” and “comments”
  • Click “OK” (The new instance of “overtime work demo” business process will be created and the employee (gaiua) will receive task.)
  1. Click “Logout”
  2. Login as employee, selected on the start form (gaiua)
  3. Click the “make a decision” task
  4. Choose “accept” or “reject” (e.g. “accept”), fill in the “comments” field and click “OK”
  5. Logout
  6. Log in as the actor, which started the process (julius)
  7. Click on task “Notify of acceptance”
  8. By clicking “OK” you’ll finish the process

Note. To monitor process click on “Process Instances” menu item and then click on instance.
Note. To see the business process graph click on “Process Definitions” menu item and click on process properties.
